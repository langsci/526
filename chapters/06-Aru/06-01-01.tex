\sscp{Merger of *j/*R}{06-01-01}
\tsc{Aru} languages share a merger of PMP *j/*R.
\citet{Nivens2017} reconstructs Proto-Aru **R for the result of this merger.
Proto-Aru **R is distinct from **r (a reflex of PMP *d).
All \tsc{Aru} languages have **r = \it{r},
and all \tsc{South Aru} languages (\srf{06-02}) also have **R
> \it{r}, thus resulting in a merger of *j/*R/*d > \it{r} for that node.
However, four languages keep the reflexes of **R distinct from **r.
Ujir has **R > \it{r,
ʤ} word initially and **R > \it{y,} {\0} word medially and finally.
Kompane, \tsc{Kola}, and Lola all have **R > \it{h} word initially
and medially, and have word-final **R > \it{h,} {\0} /{\gap}{\#}.
Examples of word-initial/medial PMP *j/*R > **R are given in
\trf{06-04}, and examples in word-final position are given in \trf{06-05}.
