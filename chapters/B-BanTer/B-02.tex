\footnotetext{We thank a reviewer for pointing out that West Damer \it{uss-o}
		and Dawera-Daweloor \it{(h)urs-ol} are reflexes of *punti,
		with a reflex of \tsc{Peripheral Babar} **d (from PMP *s/*nt)
		conditioned by the following vowel,
		which had undergone fortition to \it{s}.
		That is: *punti > Proto-Babar **uʈi > **udi > Proto-Peripheral Babar **uds.
		The same changes are seen in *isi ‘flesh’ > **iʈi > **idi > **ids
		> West Damer \it{iss-o}
		and Dawera-Daweloor \it{irs-el-ol}, ‘meat,
		flesh’.}
