\sscp{\tsc{Greater West Bomberai} (including \tsc{Timor-Alor-Pantar})}{C-10-01}
The following languages are all Papuan languages of the \tsc{Greater West Bomberai}
within which we include \tsc{Timor-Alor-Pantar}.
The islands of Alor
and Pantar are outside the range of marsupials.
Similarly, cuscus are the only marsupial present on Timor.
