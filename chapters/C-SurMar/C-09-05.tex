\footnotetext{
		The Mamuju form in \citet[10]{Stokhof1984} is given typographically as ‹koesoe›.
		The final ‹o› is presumably a (typographical) error.
		Updating the (Dutch-based) orthography without the final ‹o›
		gives us corrected \it{kuse},
		which is consistent with the rest of the \tsc{South Sulawesi}
		data and also the form in \citet[72]{Stokhof1987}.}
