\sscp{Sugar glider}{C-03-04}
\citet{Nivens2017} reconstructs Proto-Aru **la[dŋ]i `sugarglider \it{Petaurus breviceps}'.
The Malay gloss in \citet{Nivens2017} is ‘sejenis kuskus hitam
yang lincah meloncat di atas pohon’ = ‘kind of black cuscus which
leaps with agility between tree tops’.
