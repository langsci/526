\scp{Sulawesi}{C-09}
Two main kinds of cuscus are found on Sulawesi
and nearby islands: the larger bear cuscus (\it{Ailurops ursinus}
on the Sulawesi mainland,
\it{Ailurops melanotis} in the Talaud Islands),
and the smaller dwarf cuscus \it{(Strigocuscus celebensis)}.
A distinct species of dwarf cuscus \it{(Strigocuscus pelengensis)}
is found on the Banggai Islands
and nearby Sula Islands in Maluku.

Where two terms occur the first designates the bear cuscus
and the second the dwarf cuscus.
