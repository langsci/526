\sscp{\tsc{Celebic}}{C-09-04}
The following languages are all Austronesian of the \tsc{Celebic}
subgroup (following \citealp{Mead2003a,Zobel2020}).
Michael Martens reconstructs Proto-Celebic **kuse ‘bear cuscus’
and **taŋali ‘dwarf cuscus’.
We can also reconstruct Proto-Muna-Buton **mantəR ‘bear
cuscus’ (\srf{14-04}).
