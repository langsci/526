\sscp{Other terms}{C-08-04}
Words given by \citet[237]{SmitsVoorhoeve1992a} under their ambiguous
‘marsupial’ entry are given below.
When a term has a more specific identification in another source (e.g.
Yeresiam \it{átóòrà} ‘cuscus’) it is not repeated here.
Most terms in this entry which have been
identified are ‘cuscus’. However, some are ‘bandicoot’,
while others appear to be cognate with terms for ‘(tree) kangaroo’.
