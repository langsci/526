\scp{Previous classifications}{07-01}
In the following sections we examine existing proposals in the
literature regarding the classification of the languages of central Maluku.
Our focus is on the two most detailed studies of this region,
as represented by \citet{Stresemann1927} in \srf{07-01-01}
and the many works of James Collins in \srf{07-01-02}.
We also discuss other proposals in \srf{07-01-03}.
