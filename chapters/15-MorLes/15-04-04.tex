\sscp{Words limited to Wallacea and extending out to Oceania}{15-04-04}
Some words appear to be found in Wallacean subgroups
and extend out into Oceania.
They are not found west or north of the Blust line (as far as is known).
This distribution of certain words was one of the main factors
that led \citet{Blust1982,Blust1993,Blust2009,Blust2012b} to propose the CEMP subgroup.
As we have discussed them,
words like **mantəR, **kVndoR(a),
*kəRa(ŋ), *oliq, and others are not what they seemed to have
been at the time,
and no longer provide support for the CEMP hypothesis.
There are others, some of which are given in \trf{15-07},
and we discuss some of the more promising ones in the following
subsections.
