\sscp{\tsc{Sumba-Havu} phonology}{03-03-01}
\tsc{Sumba-Havu} languages have a number of phonological properties
that require discussion in order to properly understand their historical phonology.
Some of these properties are similar to those associated with
schwa in other \tsc{\ili{Bima}-Lembata} languages (\srf{03-02-01}),
while others are unique to \tsc{Sumba-Havu}.
We discuss those issues relevant to \tsc{Sumba}
followed by those relevant to \tsc{\ili{Havu}-Dhao}.
