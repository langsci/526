\scp{*mantəR}{14-04}
\citet{Blust1982,Blust2012b} glossed PCEMP **mans(aə)r as ‘bandicoot’.
In this section we discuss issues with glosses associated with
this family of forms (\srf{14-04-01}),
the distribution of reflexes (\srf{14-04-02}),
why **mantəR accounts for the data better than **mans(aə)r (\srf{14-04-03}),
forms in Papuan language families similar to **mantəR (\srf{14-04-04}),
and implications of a more complete picture
and corrected gloss of **mantəR ‘cuscus’ (\srf{14-04-05}).
