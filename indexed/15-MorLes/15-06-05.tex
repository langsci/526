\sscp{Secondary Papuan migrations from Timor}{15-06-05}
\ili{As} mentioned in \srf{02-01},
there are groups speaking Papuan languages on \ili{Alor}
and \ili{Pantar} who see their origins as autochthonous to those islands.
They say they have always been there.
There are other groups on \ili{Alor}
and \ili{Pantar} who trace their origins to a migration from the Timor mainland.
\ili{As} also mentioned in \srf{02-01},
speakers of the Papuan language of \ili{Oirata} spoken on \ili{Kisar} trace
their origins to a migration from the Timor mainland.
This possibly happened in historical times.
