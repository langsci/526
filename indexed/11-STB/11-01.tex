\scp{Changes defining \tsc{Seram-Tanimbar-Bomberai}}{11-01}
There are two sound changes shared by STB: *d/*z > **d (at l\ili{east}
word initially), and *ə/*a/*-ay/*-aw > \it{a} in final syllables.
Neither of these changes are exclusively shared innovations
and thus the evidence for STB must be considered weak.
It may be better to identify the constituent branches of this
group; \tsc{East Seram}, \tsc{Eastern Islands}, \tsc{\ili{Teor}-Kur}, and \tsc{Tanimbar-Bomberai}
as coordinate branches of MP.
More data is needed for several of these languages to properly
assess their relationships to one another
and to other languages of the region.
