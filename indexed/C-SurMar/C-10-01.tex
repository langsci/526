\sscp{\tsc{Greater West Bomberai} (including \tsc{Timor-\ili{Alor}-Pantar})}{C-10-01}
The following languages are all Papuan languages of the \tsc{Greater West Bomberai}
within which we include \tsc{Timor-\ili{Alor}-Pantar}.
The islands of \ili{Alor}
and \ili{Pantar} are outside the range of marsupials.
Similarly, cuscus are the only marsupial present on Timor.
