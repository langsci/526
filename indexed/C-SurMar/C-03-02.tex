\sscp{Bandicoots \it{(Peramelidae)}}{C-03-02}
Terms from \citet{Nivens2017} occur under the headword **koyi
the definition of which is given as: “mouse?? Or: an edible marsupial
rodent with a short tail
and black fur: long-nosed bandicoot”,
and a note “Check species of all data in this record!!!”.
It is possible that some of these terms designate \it{Dasyuridae},
which are smaller than bandicoots.
Several individual terms are glossed with \ili{Malay} \it{tikus tanah}
‘bandicoot’ (literally ‘earth rat/mouse’).
Terms from \citet{Schapper2011} are identified as \it{Echymipera
rufescens} ‘long nosed bandicoot’.
